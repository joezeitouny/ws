Services

  • Electrical System Installation: The process of installing new electrical systems or components on your boat, such as batteries, wiring, switches, and panels. This service can range from adding a single component to completely revamping the entire system.
  • Battery Replacement/Installation: Replacing old or worn-out batteries with new ones that are better suited for marine use. This includes both starter and deep cycle batteries.
  • Lighting Upgrade: Upgrading existing boat lighting to LED lights, which provide a brighter and more energy-efficient option compared to traditional incandescent bulbs.
  • Electrical Diagnostic Testing: A thorough examination of your boat's electrical system to identify potential problems or areas that could be improved. This can help prevent unexpected breakdowns while also improving efficiency and safety.
  • Wiring Installation/Repair: Adding new wiring, replacing damaged wiring, or repairing existing connections to ensure a safe and efficient electrical system.
  • Electrical Panel Upgrades/Repairs: Upgrading or repairing the boat's electrical panel (fuse box) which is essential for power distribution.
  • Solar Power System Installation: Installing solar panels to harness energy from the sun, which can help power your boat without relying solely on batteries.
  • Naval Systems Integration: Integrate navigation systems, radar, GPS, AIS, and other navico and Raymarine electronics with your electrical system. This might involve connecting these devices to the main power supply or ensuring they function correctly in marine environments.
  • Lithium Battery Installation and Maintenance: Installing lithium batteries, known for their long lifespan and high energy density, which can provide a more efficient and sustainable source of electricity on your boat.
  • Victron Hardware Integration: Installing Victron hardware products that offer advanced control and monitoring capabilities over various electrical systems onboard your vessel.
  • Marine Wiring Upgrades: Upgrading or replacing existing wiring to meet the latest standards for marine environments, ensuring optimal performance and safety.
  • Electrical System Troubleshooting: Identifying and resolving issues with your boat's electrical system, whether they involve wiring problems, faulty components, or other malfunctions.
