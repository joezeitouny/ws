Experienced boat electrician in Nydri, Lefkada Greece

As an experienced boat electrician, I specialize in installing and maintaining electrical systems on marine vessels. My expertise lies in integrating lithium batteries, solar panels, and Victron hardware to optimize power efficiency and sustainability for boats of all sizes. I also provide comprehensive services such as wiring and troubleshooting, electrical upgrades, and safety equipment installation and testing. Whether you need a complete electrical overhaul or a simple repair, I am committed to delivering top-notch service and ensuring your boat remains safe and functional on the water.

Services

Installation and troubleshooting Victron hardware, ensuring seamless integration with the rest of the systems on board

Installation, troubleshooting, and configuration of lithium-based electrical systems, providing comprehensive support for clients in optimizing their boat's power usage and reducing environmental impact
