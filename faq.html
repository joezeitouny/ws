  • Q. Which type of solar panel is more efficient: flexible or rigid?
    A. Rigid solar panels tend to be more efficient than flexible ones. They generally use monocrystalline or polycrystalline cells, which offer higher efficiency rates. Flexible panels often use thin-film technology, which is less efficient, although advancements in materials are closing this gap.
  • Q. Are flexible solar panels more expensive than rigid ones?
    A. Flexible solar panels tend to be more expensive per watt of power generated compared to rigid panels. This is due to the materials and technology used to make them flexible and lightweight. However, their lower weight and ease of installation can reduce overall installation costs.
